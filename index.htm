Essential Gear

  • Comfortable workout clothes
  • Training shoes suitable for gym activities
  • Water bottle
  • Gym towel
  • Resistance bands
  • Weightlifting gloves (optional)

Nutrition Basics

A proper diet is key to making progress at the gym. Here are some guidelines:

  • Eat a balanced diet with protein, carbs, and healthy fats
  • Stay hydrated throughout the day
  • Pre-workout meals: opt for something light but energizing
  • Post-workout recovery: focus on protein-rich meals

Beginner Exercises

Here are some basic exercises to get started:

  • Squats
  • Push-ups
  • Planks
  • Lunges
  • Dumbbell rows
  • Jumping jacks (warm-up)

Tips for Beginners

  • Start slow and focus on form
  • Warm up before and stretch after each session
  • Track your progress (reps, sets, weight)
  • Rest and recovery are as important as workouts
  • Ask for help from trainers or experienced gym-goers
